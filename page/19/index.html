Some positive integers nnn have the property that the sum [ nnn + reverse(nnn) ] consists entirely of odd (decimal) digits. For instance, 36+63=9936 + 63 = 9936+63=99 and 409+904=1313409 + 904 = 1313409+904=1313. We will call such numbers reversible; so 36,63,40936, 63, 40936,63,409, and 904904904 are reversible. Leading zeroes are not allowed in either nnn or reverse(nnn).

There are 120120120 reversible numbers below one-thousand.

How many reversible numbers are there below one-billion (10910^9109)?

在小于一千的数中，一共有 120120120 个可逆数。

在小于十亿（10910^9109）的数中，一共有多少个可逆数？
