problems

Peter has nine four-sided (pyramidal) dice, each with faces numbered 1,2,3,41, 2, 3, 41,2,3,4.

Colin has six six-sided (cubic) dice, each with faces numbered 1,2,3,4,5,61, 2, 3, 4, 5, 61,2,3,4,5,6.

Peter and Colin roll their dice and compare totals: the highest total wins. The result is a draw if the totals are equal.

What is the probability that Pyramidal Pete beats Cubic Colin? Give your answer rounded to seven decimal places in the form 0.abcdefg0.abcdefg0.abcdefg

translation

Peter 有 999 个四面的骰子，每个面上分别标着 1,2,3,41,2,3,41,2,3,4。

Colin 有 666 个六面的骰子，每个面上分别标着 1,2,3,4,5,61,2,3,4,5,61,2,3,4,5,6。

他们分别摇骰子，然后比较大小：分数高的获胜，如果分数相同就平局。

求问 Peter 获胜的概率。将你的答案保留七位小数按照 0.abcdefg0.abcdefg0.abcdefg 的格式输出。
