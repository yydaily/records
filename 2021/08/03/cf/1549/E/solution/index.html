C. The Three Little Pigs

开启传送门

题目描述

Three little pigs from all over the world are meeting for a convention! Every minute, a triple of 3 new pigs arrives on the convention floor. After the nnn-th minute, the convention ends.

The big bad wolf has learned about this convention, and he has an attack plan. At some minute in the convention, he will arrive and eat exactly xxx pigs. Then he will get away.

The wolf wants Gregor to help him figure out the number of possible attack plans that involve eating exactly xxx pigs for various values of xxx (1≤x≤3n1\le x\le 3n1≤x≤3n). Two attack plans are considered different, if they occur at different times or if the sets of little pigs to eat are different.

Note that all queries are independent, that is, the wolf does not eat the little pigs, he only makes plans!

Input

The first line of input contains two integers nnn and qqq (1≤n≤106,1≤q≤2⋅1051\le n\le 10^6, 1\le q\le 2⋅10^51≤n≤106,1≤q≤2⋅105), the number of minutes the convention lasts and the number of queries the wolf asks.

Each of the next qqq lines contains a single integer xix_ixi​ (1≤xi≤3n1\le x_i\le 3n1≤xi​≤3n), the number of pigs the wolf will eat in the iii-th query.

Output

You should print qqq lines, with line iii representing the number of attack plans if the wolf wants to eat xix_ixi​ pigs. Since each query answer can be large, output each answer modulo 109+710^9+7109+7.

Examples

input1

2 3

1

5

6

output1

9

6

1

input2

5 4

2

4

6

8

output2

225

2001

6014

6939

Note

In the example test, n=2n=2n=2. Thus, there are 333 pigs at minute 111, and 666 pigs at minute 222. There are three queries: x=1,x=5x=1, x=5x=1,x=5, and x=6x=6x=6.

If the wolf wants to eat 111 pig, he can do so in 3+6=93+6=93+6=9 possible attack plans, depending on whether he arrives at minute 111 or 222.

If the wolf wants to eat 555 pigs, the wolf cannot arrive at minute 111, since there aren’t enough pigs at that time. Therefore, the wolf has to arrive at minute 222, and there are 666 possible attack plans.

If the wolf wants to eat 666 pigs, his only plan is to arrive at the end of the convention and devour everybody.

Remember to output your answers modulo 109+710^9+7109+7!

题意

给定 nnn 和 qqq，一共 qqq 次询问，每次询问一个 xix_ixi​, 每次回答 ∑i=1nC3∗ixi\sum_{i=1}^n C_{3*i}^{x_i}∑i=1n​C3∗ixi​​

分析

我们定义函数 P(x)=∑i=1n(1+x)3iP(x) = \sum_{i=1}^n (1+x)^{3i}P(x)=∑i=1n​(1+x)3i, 那我们有 xix^ixi 对应的系数，就是答案。

由于 P(x)P(x)P(x) 是个等比数列，我们可以化简为：

P(x)=(1+x)3n+3−(1+x)3(1+x)3−1P(x) = \frac{(1+x)^{3n+3}-(1+x)^3}{(1+x)^3-1} P(x)=(1+x)3−1(1+x)3n+3−(1+x)3​

然后我们就可以预处理快速求出所有的系数.
