E. Non-Decreasing Dilemma

开启传送门

题目描述

Alice has recently received an array a1,a2,…,ana_1, a_2, \dots, a_na1​,a2​,…,an​ for her birthday! She is very proud of her array, and when she showed her friend Bob the array, he was very happy with her present too!

However, soon Bob became curious, and as any sane friend would do, asked Alice to perform qqq operations of two types on her array:

  • 1 x y1\ x\ y1 x y: update the element axa_xax​ to yyy (set ax=ya_x=yax​=y).
  • 2 l r2\ l\ r2 l r: calculate how many non-decreasing subarrays exist within the subarray [al,al+1,…,ar][a_l, a_{l+1}, \dots, a_r][al​,al+1​,…,ar​]. More formally, count the number of pairs of integers (p,q)(p,q)(p,q) such that l≤p≤q≤rl\le p\le q\le rl≤p≤q≤r and ap≤ap+1≤dots≤qq−1≤aqa_p\le a_{p+1}\le dots\le q_{q-1}\le a_qap​≤ap+1​≤dots≤qq−1​≤aq​.
    Help Alice answer Bob’s queries!

Input

The first line contains two integers nnn and qqq (1≤n,q≤2⋅1051 \le n, q \le 2 \cdot 10^51≤n,q≤2⋅105) — the size of the array, and the number of queries, respectively.

The second line contains nnn integers a1,a2,…,ana_1, a_2, \dots, a_na1​,a2​,…,an​ (1≤ai≤1091 \le a_i \le 10^91≤ai​≤109) — the elements of Alice’s array.

The next qqq lines consist of three integers each. The first integer of the iii-th line is tit_iti​, the operation being performed on the iii-th step (ti=1t_i = 1ti​=1 or ti=2t_i = 2ti​=2).

If ti=1t_i = 1ti​=1, the next two integers are xix_ixi​ and yiy_iyi​ (1≤xi≤n1 \le x_i \le n1≤xi​≤n; 1≤yi≤1091 \le y_i \le 10^91≤yi​≤109), updating the element at position xix_ixi​ to yiy_iyi​ (setting axi=yia_{x_i} = y_iaxi​​=yi​).

If ti=2t_i = 2ti​=2, the next two integers are lil_ili​ and rir_iri​ (1≤li≤ri≤n1 \le l_i \le r_i \le n1≤li​≤ri​≤n), the two indices Bob asks Alice about for the iii-th query.

It’s guaranteed that there is at least one operation of the second type.

Output

For each query of type 222, print a single integer, the answer to the query.

Example

Input1

5 6
3 1 4 1 5
2 2 5
2 1 3
1 4 4
2 2 5
1 2 6
2 2 5

Output1

6
4
10
7

Note

For the first query, l=2l=2l=2 and r=5r=5r=5, and the non-decreasing subarrays [p,q][p,q][p,q] are [2,2],[3,3],[4,4],[5,5],[2,3][2,2], [3,3], [4,4], [5,5], [2,3][2,2],[3,3],[4,4],[5,5],[2,3] and [4,5][4,5][4,5].

题意

给你一个序列，每次给你一个操作，操作包括两种：修改 & 询问。

修改：每次将序列中一个数字改为 yyy

询问：每次问你一个区间内有多少个不下降子序列。

分析

看题目描述也显然是线段树瞎搞。

显然我们需要维护三个东西：当前区间的价值，当前区间的不下降子序列前缀最长为多少，当前区间的不下降子序列后缀最长多少。

然后每次修改的时候，我们只需要维护对应路径上的节点即可。

稍微需要注意的就是，区间合并的时候，如果右区间的左端点和左区间的右端点满足对应的大小关系，那么说明这两个区间是可以合并的，合并的时候需要更新对应的value，并且可能会影响当前区间的前缀，后缀长度。
