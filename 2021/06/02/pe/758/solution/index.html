There are 333 buckets labelled SSS (small) of 333 litres, MMM (medium) of 555 litres and LLL (large) of 888 litres.

Initially SSS and MMM are full of water and LLL is empty. By pouring water between the buckets exactly one litre of water can be measured.

Since there is no other way to measure, once a pouring starts it cannot stop until either the source bucket is empty or the destination bucket is full.

At least four pourings are needed to get one litre:

(3,5,0)⟶M→L(3,0,5)⟶S→M(0,3,5)⟶L→S(3,3,2)⟶S→M(1,5,2)(3,5,0) \stackrel{M \rightarrow L}{\longrightarrow}(3,0,5) \stackrel{S \rightarrow M}{\longrightarrow}(0,3,5) \stackrel{L \rightarrow S}{\longrightarrow}(3,3,2) \stackrel{S \rightarrow M}{\longrightarrow}(1,5,2) (3,5,0)⟶M→L​(3,0,5)⟶S→M​(0,3,5)⟶L→S​(3,3,2)⟶S→M​(1,5,2)

After these operations, there is exactly one litre in bucket SSS.

In general the sizes of the buckets S,M,LS,M,LS,M,L are aaa, bbb, a+ba+ba+b litres, respectively. Initially SSS and MMM are full and LLL is empty. If the above rule of pouring still applies and aaa and bbb are two coprime positive integers with a≤ba\le ba≤b then it is always possible to measure one litre in finitely many steps.

Let P(a,b)P(a,b)P(a,b) be the minimal number of pourings needed to get one litre. Thus P(3,5)=4P(3,5)=4P(3,5)=4.
Also, P(7,31)=20P(7,31)=20P(7,31)=20 and P(1234,4321)=2780P(1234,4321)=2780P(1234,4321)=2780.

Find the sum of P(2p5−1,2q5−1)P(2^{p^5}-1, 2^{q^{5}}-1)P(2p5−1,2q5−1) for all pairs of prime numbers p,qp,qp,q such that p<q<1000p \lt q \lt 1000p<q<1000.
Give your answer modulo 1 000 000 0071\ 000\ 000\ 0071 000 000 007.

translation

有三个桶，分别标记为 容量为 333 升的小桶，容量为 555 升的中桶，容量为 888 升的大桶。

开始的时候，小桶和中桶都装满了水，但是大桶是空的。通过把水在两个桶中相互倒，我们最终能准确得到 111 升水。

由于没有其他的测量工具，所以在一次倒水过程中，要么倒水的桶被倒空，要么接水的桶接满。否则倒水过程就不能终止。

在上述的样例中，我们至少需要通过四次倒水才能得到 111 升水，方案如下：

(3,5,0)⟶中→大(3,0,5)⟶小→中(0,3,5)⟶大→小(3,3,2)⟶小→中(1,5,2)(3,5,0) \stackrel{中 \rightarrow 大}{\longrightarrow}(3,0,5) \stackrel{小 \rightarrow 中}{\longrightarrow}(0,3,5) \stackrel{大 \rightarrow 小}{\longrightarrow}(3,3,2) \stackrel{小 \rightarrow 中}{\longrightarrow}(1,5,2) (3,5,0)⟶中→大​(3,0,5)⟶小→中​(0,3,5)⟶大→小​(3,3,2)⟶小→中​(1,5,2)

通过这些操作后，小桶中就刚好有 111 升水。

如果我们将大中小桶的容积改成 a,b,a+ba, b, a+ba,b,a+b。初始的时候，小桶和中桶是满的，但是大桶是空的。通过上述规则倒水，在 a,ba,ba,b 是互质的时候，我们总是能通过反复倒水得到 111 升水。

定义 $P(a, b) $ 为量出 111 升水的最少步数。因此有 P(3,5)=4,P(7,31)=20,P(1234,4321)=2780P(3,5)=4, P(7, 31) = 20, P(1234,4321) = 2780P(3,5)=4,P(7,31)=20,P(1234,4321)=2780。

令 p,qp, qp,q 为满足 p<q<1000p\lt q\lt 1000p<q<1000 的质数对，求所有这样质数对P(2p5−1,2q5−1)P(2^{p^5}-1, 2^{q^{5}}-1)P(2p5−1,2q5−1) 的和 膜上1 000 000 0071\ 000\ 000\ 0071 000 000 007.
