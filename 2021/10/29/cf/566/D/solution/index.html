D. Restructuring Company

开启传送门

题目描述

Even the most successful company can go through a crisis period when you have to make a hard decision — to restructure, discard and merge departments, fire employees and do other unpleasant stuff. Let’s consider the following model of a company.

There are nnn people working for the Large Software Company. Each person belongs to some department. Initially, each person works on his own project in his own department (thus, each company initially consists of nnn departments, one person in each).

However, harsh times have come to the company and the management had to hire a crisis manager who would rebuild the working process in order to boost efficiency. Let’s use team(person)team(person)team(person) to represent a team where person personpersonperson works. A crisis manager can make decisions of two types:

  1. Merge departments team(x)team(x)team(x) and team(y)team(y)team(y) into one large department containing all the employees of team(x)team(x)team(x) and team(y)team(y)team(y), where xxx and yyy (1 ≤ x, y le n1 \le x, y le n1 ≤ x, y le n) — are numbers of two of some company employees. If team(x)team(x)team(x) matches team(y)team(y)team(y), then nothing happens.
  2. Merge departments team(x), team(x + 1), …, team(y)team(x), team(x + 1), \dots, team(y)team(x), team(x + 1), …, team(y), where xxx and yyy (1 ≤ x ≤ y ≤ n1 \le x \le y \le n1 ≤ x ≤ y ≤ n) — the numbers of some two employees of the company.

At that the crisis manager can sometimes wonder whether employees xxx and yyy (1≤x,y≤n1 \le x, y \le n1≤x,y≤n) work at the same department.

Help the crisis manager and answer all of his queries.

If type=3type = 3type=3, then your task is to determine whether employees xxx and yyy work at the same department. Note that xxx can be equal to yyy in the query of any type.

For each question of type 333 print “YES” or “NO” (without the quotes), depending on whether the corresponding people work in the same department.

题意

一共有 nnn 个人，一开始的时候，每个人的老大都是自己。
现在有 qqq 次操作，操作一共分三种。

  1. 让工号为 aaa 的打工人的老大 变成 bbb
  2. 让工号为 [a,b][a,b][a,b] 的一群打工人的老大变成 bbb
  3. 问你 aaa 和 bbb 的顶头上司是不是一个人。

分析

一眼dsu，然后莽一发，果不其然，TLE on case 4。然后一看代码，没关流同步，然后果断关了再交，TLE on case 19

理性分析一波。

首先如果只有操作 111 和操作 333 ，那么显然一眼dsu。

如果只有操作 222 和操作 333，那么显然一眼线段树，或者 set<pair<int,int>> 合并，或者用各种奇怪的方式写个线段并就行了。

难点在于 111 和 222 同时存在的时候，如何优雅的更新，并保证复杂度。

简单思考一下dsu的本质，就是通过关联节点，保证一个节点不会更新太多次。

同理，我们是否可以通过关联线段，来保证一个线段里面的节点不会更新太多次呢？

当然可以，一个经典做法是，我们记录每个点关联的右端点是谁，也即更新本节点后，只需要更新本节点的右端点即可。这样可以保证，每次节点和区间更新的时候，节点会不重复的更新。也即，每次merge保证了最少把一个节点merge到新节点上了。
